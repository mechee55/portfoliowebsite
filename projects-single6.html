Client #3

LALA'S Argentine Grill

LALA'S is an established, popular restaurant with 4 locations all over Los Angeles.

Their customers LOVE their chimichurri, but didn't know that LALA'S had been selling it by the jar for years. I thought it was a problem that could be fixed easily. The flyer was placed inside each check holder so the customers were FORCED to look at it! The results were immediate, and the sales has since increased by almost 40%.

For their NYE special course menu, I did the photoshoot, as well as facebook and instagram paid advertisements.

LALA'S had another problem to be fixed; their unknown awesome happy hour. My solution was a detachable and durable blackboard-like poster for each location to be displayed from 4pm to 7pm.
