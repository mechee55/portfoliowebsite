Design Projects

Product Design - mockups

The most challenging part of these school projects were the very specific requirements I needed to fulfill in order to pass. At the same time they gave me clearer ideas to my designs. I received an A+ on both projects.

For the book cover set, I used the triadic color scheme, then the monochromatic color scheme on each cover. I included an illustration of the mountain to convey conceptual unity across the set.

The album cover was fun to make. One Ok Rock is my favorite Japanese band that's been touring all over the world. Instead of redesigning their existing album cover, I came up with this fictitious album title, and wove the title together to contrast the script typeface with the block letters.

School: New York Institute of Art and Design
Skills: Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
