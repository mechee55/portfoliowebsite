Design Project #2

Social Media Ads - mockups

Disclaimer: These campaigns are 100% fictional. American Airlines are not a sponsor of the Olympics.

These projects also had specific technical and design requirements. We see ads like these all the time, but actually creating it was a lot more challenging than I expected.

In this fictional Olympics campaign, my goal was to attract people to fly with American Airlines to Japan to see the sports added to the 2020 Olympics; Baseball, softball, skateboarding, surfing, karate and sports climbing.

In the Target campaign, I had to make 5 social media ads using the templates provided by the school, as well as different complementary color schemes for each ad. I used a gradient map adjustment layer to apply the same visual style across all ads.

School: New York Institute of Art and Design
Skills: Photoshop
